Common FAQ'S

Vehicle Recovery Information

How quickly can you reach me in an emergency?

For locations within Greater Glasgow, we typically arrive within 45 minutes. During peak hours or adverse weather conditions, response times may vary. We prioritize emergency situations and always keep you updated on our estimated arrival time.

What areas do you cover?

We cover all of Greater Glasgow including surrounding areas such as Paisley, East Kilbride, Clydebank, Bearsden, Bishopbriggs, and Hamilton. We also service major motorways including the M8, M74, and M77 corridors within our coverage area.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes, we work with all major insurance companies and can handle the claims process directly with your insurer. We recommend keeping your policy details handy when you call, but we can still assist you even if you don't have them immediately available.

What types of vehicles do you recover?

We recover all types of vehicles including cars, motorcycles, vans, and light commercial vehicles. Our fleet is equipped to handle everything from standard vehicles to luxury cars and classic automobiles, ensuring appropriate equipment and expertise for each recovery.
